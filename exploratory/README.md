# Exploratory sketches — not results

These documents are speculative founding drafts toward a "mathematics of
changing laws" (metadynamical geometry), produced during the fold project's
ideation. **They contain no theorems and make no claims.** Their terms have not
earned their place by producing computable examples or proofs, which is the bar
a genuine primitive must clear (see the discipline stated in each file).

They are included for provenance and because the ideation that produced the fold
results also produced these — but a reader should treat them as questions and
vocabulary experiments, never as findings. The actual results are in
[`../contribution/`](../contribution/).

| `ELEMENTS.md` | Euclid-style seed: definitions before metaphors, provisional axioms |
| `METADYNAMICAL_GEOMETRY.md` | State and governing law as joint coordinates |
| `ARCHITECTURE.md` | Structural sketch |
| `LANGUAGE.md` | Vocabulary experiments |
| `LENSES.md` | Reframings considered |
